Project Purpose

The German Bridge website provides a centralized platform for German language learners to find structured course information, access learning materials, and connect with a community of fellow students. It aims to bridge the gap between solo studying and interactive classroom learning.

Target Audience

International students, expatriates living in Germany, and language enthusiasts (A1-C1 levels) looking for both resources and a community of like-minded learners.
